Representatives of the nuclear industry in OECD nations issued the following communiqué at the inaugural Government-Industry “Roadmaps to New Nuclear” conference organized by the OECD Nuclear Energy Agency (NEA) and the Government of France, in Paris on September 28-29, 2023. The communiqué is availableas a pdf.

At the same meeting a Joint Communiqué was issued by the energy ministers and heads of delegations of Bulgaria, Canada, Czech Republic, Estonia, Finland, France, Ghana, Hungary, Japan, Korea, Poland, Romania, Netherlands, Slovakia, Slovenia, Sweden, Türkiye, Ukraine, The United Kingdom an. the United States. That statement is also availableas a pdf.
